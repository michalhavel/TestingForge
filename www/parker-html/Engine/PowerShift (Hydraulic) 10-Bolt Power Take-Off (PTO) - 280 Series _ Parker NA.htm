Chelsea 280 Series is a hydraulically shifted hot shift PTO designed for automatic transmissions with a 10-Bolt aperture, commonly found on Allison and Caterpiller transmissions. The 280 Series replaces the 277/278 Series.

Product Overview
  • Print Product Overview
Description
    Chelsea's 280 Series is a new PTO that delivers high continuous-duty torque in a range of speeds where power and durability are required.  It is capable of up to 390 ft-lbs in continuous duty applications. With nine ratios, rotatable output flanges, remote hydraulic valve options, wet-spline technology, and Chelsea Smart Start options, it is perfect for Vacuum, Refuse, and Fire & Rescue applications in the Mobile market.      

    Markets:
    • Aerial
    • Oil & Gas
    • Defense
    • On-Highway

    Features/Benefits:
    • Rotatable Flanges to improve clearance
    • Continuous-Duty Torque Rating up to 390 ft-lbs
    • Wet-Spline Option to extend PTO/Pump shaft life
    • Smart Start Option for High-Torque Start-up
    • Two Year Warranty

    Applications:
    • Fire & Rescue Trucks
    • Pumper Cleaner
    • Truck Cranes
    • Refuse Trucks
